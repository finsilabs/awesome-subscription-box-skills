# Meta Ads Launch Brief for PetalBox

## Problem/Feature Description

PetalBox is a monthly subscription box for flower-arranging enthusiasts, shipping a curated selection of seasonal blooms, floral foam, and design accessories. The founders are preparing to launch their first paid social campaign on Meta (Facebook and Instagram) to drive new subscriptions. They have no prior ad history and are starting from scratch with a $3,000/month budget.

The campaign objective is to acquire new subscribers at a target CPA under $45. The audience is women aged 28-50 in the US who follow floral design accounts, home décor influencers, and lifestyle brands. No brand voice vault exists yet.

The campaign is planned to run for 6 weeks starting next month with no competitive constraints, though the founders prefer an aspirational tone over a price-focused one.

Produce a complete campaign creative brief for this launch campaign. Save it as `briefs/petalbox-launch.md`.

- `briefs/petalbox-launch.md` — the full campaign creative brief
