# Homepage Build for MoonBean Coffee Co.

## Problem/Feature Description

MoonBean Coffee Co. is a specialty coffee subscription box launching next quarter. Subscribers choose from three tiers — Drip (entry), Pour Over (mid), and Espresso Enthusiast (premium) — and receive a curated monthly box of single-origin coffees, brewing accessories, and barista extras. The marketing team has approved the brand copy and the development lead needs a complete Shopify Dawn-theme homepage ready to hand off for review before pushing to the staging store.

The store is running Dawn. The templates/, sections/, and assets/ directories already exist. What's needed is a production-ready `templates/index.json` that captures the full customer journey on the homepage: an emotional hook up top, an explanation of how the service works, a tier picker where subscribers can choose their plan, a gallery of recent box contents, customer testimonials, answers to common questions, and finally an email capture for visitors who aren't ready to subscribe yet.

## Output Specification

Produce `templates/index.json` — a complete Dawn-compatible homepage template for MoonBean Coffee Co. The JSON should include all the sections that guide a visitor from first impression through to conversion. Place it in the current working directory as `templates/index.json`.

Do not modify any other files for this task. Name the tiers Drip ($39/mo), Pour Over ($65/mo), and Espresso Enthusiast ($95/mo). Make up realistic but fitting copy for all sections.
