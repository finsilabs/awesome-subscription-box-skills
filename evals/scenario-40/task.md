# Meta Campaign Brief for RunBox Athletic Subscription

## Problem/Feature Description

RunBox is a monthly subscription box for runners delivering curated training gear, race fuel, and recovery products. The brand is entering its growth phase after a successful soft launch and is preparing to invest seriously in paid social for the first time. The CMO wants to run a structured campaign with clear measurable targets so the team can make data-driven scaling decisions.

The campaign will target cold audiences on Meta — runners aged 25-45, US-based, with interest signals including running events, running shoes, race training, and marathon communities. Monthly budget is $5,000. The campaign launch date is in 3 weeks. The main offer is the standard monthly subscription at $49/month.

The CMO has emphasized that the brief must include unambiguous benchmarks for success — the team needs to know specifically when to put more money behind this campaign and when to shut down an underperforming ad set. This clarity is critical because the media buyer and CMO are in different time zones and need written criteria they can reference independently.

Create a complete Meta ads brief for this acquisition campaign. Save it to `briefs/runbox-launch.md`.
