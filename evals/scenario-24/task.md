# Q4 Ads Calendar for GearHead Box

## Problem/Feature Description

GearHead Box is a monthly subscription box for motorsport enthusiasts. They have been running Meta ads for about six months — cold acquisition and retargeting — and are now gearing up for their biggest commercial period of the year. Their Head of Growth has asked for a structured Q4 paid ads calendar covering October through December.

The calendar needs to show how the team should adjust their budget week by week as they approach Black Friday/Cyber Monday and the holiday gifting window. The creative team also needs to know when to produce new ad assets — they are typically two weeks behind, so they need a schedule that tells them how often to rotate creatives for each campaign type so they can plan their workload. The team currently runs cold acquisition, retargeting, and a small brand awareness campaign.

The brand's baseline Meta monthly budget is $6,000 (split across all campaign types).

## Output Specification

Produce `q4-ads-calendar.md` in the current working directory. The calendar should include:

- A schedule of when budget should increase or decrease across the quarter, with the percentage change relative to baseline and the approximate dollar amount for each period
- A creative production schedule that tells the design team exactly how often to refresh creatives for each campaign type so they can plan asset handoffs
- An "always-on" table showing the campaigns running throughout, their daily budgets, audiences, and expected creative rotation frequency
- Notes about how the paid calendar should sync with any planned email campaigns for key moments like BFCM and holiday gifting
