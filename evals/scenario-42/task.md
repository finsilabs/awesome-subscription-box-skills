# TikTok and Reels Video Campaign Brief for WildSnack Box

## Problem/Feature Description

WildSnack Box is a monthly subscription box featuring unusual, globally-sourced snacks — unusual chips, hot sauces, fermented goods, and novelty candy. The brand is known for entertaining "reaction" content that performs well in short-form video. They are launching their first video-first paid campaign targeting Gen Z and millennial snack adventurers on TikTok and Instagram Reels through Meta.

The campaign window is 6 weeks, budget is $4,500 total. The primary format is 15-second vertical video. Target audience: adults aged 18-35 in the US who follow food content creators, mukbang channels, snack review accounts, and international cuisine.

The creative director is planning to produce three 15-second video spots: one unboxing format, one taste-reaction format, and one "what's inside" product showcase. The team needs the brief to include specific creative direction for the video format — particularly the opening hook, timing of the CTA, and the aspect ratio specs — as well as still image specs for any feed placement top-up budget.

Create a complete ads creative brief for this video campaign. Save it to `briefs/wildsnack-video.md`.
