# Behind-the-Scenes Blog Post for GearHead Box

## Problem/Feature Description

GearHead Box is a monthly subscription box for motorsport enthusiasts — track day gear, performance accessories, and automotive collectibles. Their content team wants to publish a behind-the-scenes article on their Shopify blog about how the team curates each month's box: the research process, the supplier relationships, and how they decide what makes the cut each month. The target reader is an existing subscriber who is curious about the brand story and what goes into each box.

The article is intended to be published directly to their Shopify "News" blog. The content team has asked for a complete, ready-to-publish HTML file they can paste into the Shopify article editor, along with the key metadata fields they'll need when creating the article in Shopify admin.

Target length is 700-900 words. The brand voice is direct, knowledgeable, and slightly irreverent — written for people who actually turn their own wrenches, not casual car fans.

## Output Specification

Produce two files:

1. `article.html` — the full HTML body of the article, ready to paste into the Shopify article editor's HTML view
2. `article-metadata.json` — a JSON file containing the metadata fields: `title`, `handle`, `summary`, and `tags`

The article should follow a structure that works well for brand storytelling. The HTML should use appropriate Shopify-compatible formatting conventions throughout.
