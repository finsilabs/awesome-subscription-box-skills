# Annual Paid Ads Calendar for BrewBox

## Problem/Feature Description

BrewBox is a monthly subscription box for craft beer enthusiasts, shipping 6 rotating craft beers each month with tasting notes, brewery profiles, and a pairing guide. They have been running Meta ads casually for eight months (cold acquisition and retargeting), but have never had a proper annual plan — campaigns are launched reactively whenever the marketing team thinks of one.

The Head of Growth wants to get ahead of the calendar for the next 12 months. She needs a structured annual plan she can hand to the media buyer, the creative team, and the email marketing team so everyone is working from the same schedule. The brand sends a Klaviyo email calendar that already exists at `~/.claude/brand-voice-vault/brewbox/calendar/2026.md`.

The creative team needs enough lead time to produce assets — they typically need two weeks to turn around new ad creative. The audience team refreshes lookalike and interest audiences periodically and needs a schedule for when to do that.

The brand currently spends $8,000/month in the off-peak months. They have no TikTok or Google presence yet, just Meta.

## Output Specification

Produce `annual-ads-calendar.md` in the current working directory containing the full annual paid ads calendar for BrewBox.

The calendar must include:
- An always-on section showing the campaigns that run continuously, their daily budgets, target audiences, and creative refresh schedule
- A quarterly breakdown showing when budgets change and why
- A section showing when the creative team needs to deliver new assets and which campaign they're for
- Notes on when the audience manager should update lookalike and interest audiences
- Notes on how the paid schedule relates to the email calendar
