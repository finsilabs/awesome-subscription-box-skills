# Black Friday Campaign Brief for TechKit Monthly

## Problem/Feature Description

TechKit Monthly is a subscription box for tech enthusiasts, delivering curated gadgets, accessories, and cables. They are planning a Black Friday / Cyber Monday campaign on Meta to drive a surge of new subscriptions during the biggest shopping weekend of the year. The campaign begins November 20 and runs through December 1.

The campaign budget is $8,000 for the two-week push. Target audience: adults aged 22-40, interests in technology, gadgets, smart home, and gaming. The promotional offer is 30% off the first box using the code BF2026. The designated landing page for the campaign is the Black Friday sales page at https://techkitmonthly.com/black-friday. The campaign slug for internal tracking is bf-2026.

The analytics team needs to ensure all paid traffic from this campaign is properly tracked in their attribution reports, so the UTM parameters on all links must be set up correctly.

Create a complete Meta ads brief for the Black Friday campaign. Save it to `briefs/techkit-bf.md`.
