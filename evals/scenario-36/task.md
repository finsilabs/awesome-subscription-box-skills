# Valentine's Day Campaign Brief for SweetBox Dessert Subscription

## Problem/Feature Description

SweetBox is a monthly subscription box for dessert lovers, delivering artisan chocolates, cookies, and confections from independent pastry makers across the country. They are preparing a Valentine's Day campaign targeting gift-givers (partners, family members buying gifts for significant others) on Meta.

The campaign window is January 20 – February 14. Budget is $3,500 for the campaign period. The audience is adults aged 22-45 in the US, with interest signals around gift giving, food subscriptions, and relationship occasions. The goal is gifting subscriptions, and the target CPA is under $60 per new subscriber acquired through the campaign.

The head of marketing needs copy that works across multiple placements — from feed posts to Stories to placements with description text — and wants meaningful variation to test across placements and audiences.

Create a full Meta ads brief for this Valentine's campaign. Save it to `briefs/sweetbox-valentines.md`.
