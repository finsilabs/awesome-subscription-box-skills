# Paid Social Brief for HomeScent Candle Subscription

## Problem/Feature Description

HomeScent is a monthly candle subscription box that ships two artisan candles per month, hand-poured by small-batch makers. They are launching their first paid social campaign on Meta targeting home décor enthusiasts and gift buyers. The design team is about to brief their freelance photographer and graphic designer, and they need a clear brief document that includes detailed creative direction for all the image assets required for the campaign.

The campaign runs as always-on acquisition through Q2. Budget is $2,000 per month. Target audience: women 27-50 in the US who follow home décor, interior design, and wellness accounts. Goal is subscriptions at a CPA under $50.

Produce a complete Meta ads creative brief. The design team specifically needs the asset specs section to be thorough — they will hand it directly to the creative team for asset production. Save the brief to `briefs/homescent-q2.md`.

## Output Specification

- `briefs/homescent-q2.md` — the complete creative brief
