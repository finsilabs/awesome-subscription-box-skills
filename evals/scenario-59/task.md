# Subscription Box Store Build Plan

## Problem/Feature Description

A founder has just decided to launch a subscription box business selling artisanal coffee from independent roasters around the world. The company is called "Roam & Roast" and will offer three subscription tiers (Wanderer $29/mo, Explorer $49/mo, Connoisseur $79/mo) shipped monthly. They have an existing brand website at roamandroast.com and are planning to launch as a pre-order with fulfillment starting in 8 weeks.

The founder has hired a Shopify developer who is new to subscription box stores. Your job is to produce a detailed build plan that sequences every phase of the store build correctly from start to finish, so the developer can hand it off to any team member without them needing to figure out the right order or understand the inter-phase dependencies.

## Output Specification

Produce a file called `build-plan.md` that documents the complete, ordered sequence of build phases required to launch this store. For each phase, include:
- What specialist skill or tool is invoked
- What artifact or output is produced
- Why this phase must happen before the next one (the dependency rationale)

Also include a brief dependency diagram or summary showing which later phases rely on outputs from earlier phases.
