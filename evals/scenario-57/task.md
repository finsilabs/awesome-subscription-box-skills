# Cyber Monday Sale Page — BrewCrate Coffee Subscription

## Problem/Feature Description

BrewCrate is a specialty coffee subscription box — subscribers receive single-origin whole beans and a curated brew guide each month. Their biggest acquisition window of the year is Cyber Monday, and the growth team is planning a 30% discount offer on all tiers for new subscribers who sign up November 28–December 1.

The conversion rate on last year's sale page was disappointing — the team reviewed the session recordings and found that visitors were not scrolling to the CTA section, and many who did scroll were leaving before completing a purchase. The team believes the page structure was too passive and needs to work harder throughout the visitor's journey.

This year they want a redesigned campaign page at `/pages/cyber-monday` that converts harder and handles objections before a visitor can leave. The developer has the Shopify Page record created. They need the custom JSON template.

## Output Specification

Produce `templates/page.cyber-monday.json` — a Dawn-compatible JSON template for the Cyber Monday sale landing page. The page should be structured to maximize conversion throughout the scroll depth.

Also produce `page-strategy.md` explaining the section structure chosen, where each CTA appears in the page flow, and how the page handles objections before a visitor exits.
