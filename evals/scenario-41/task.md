# Father's Day Campaign Brief for GearHeadBox

## Problem/Feature Description

GearHeadBox is a monthly subscription box for automotive enthusiasts delivering curated track-day gear, performance accessories, and detailing products. The marketing team is preparing a Father's Day gifting campaign to run May 25 through June 15. This campaign targets gift-buyers — primarily partners, adult children, and family members purchasing a gift for a car-enthusiast dad or partner.

The campaign will run on Meta with a $6,000 total budget. The marketing director has identified three distinct potential audiences:
- Gift-buyers who have visited the site but not converted (warm)
- New-to-brand gift-buyers in the US who follow automotive accounts (cold)
- Current subscribers' email contacts (uploaded lookalike seed)

The team also wants to ensure that current subscribers and recent purchasers are not targeted by the gifting campaign, since sending a "give the gift of GearHeadBox" ad to someone who already subscribes would be confusing and inefficient.

Create a complete Meta ads brief for this Father's Day gifting campaign. Save it to `briefs/ghb-fathersday.md`.
