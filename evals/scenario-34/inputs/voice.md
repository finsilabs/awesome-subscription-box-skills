# Brand Voice — NorthBrew Coffee

## Tone
Confident, knowledgeable, unpretentious. We write like a seasoned coffee geek talking to a curious friend — not a barista lecturing a customer.

## Vocabulary
DO use: "exceptional", "single-origin", "craft", "sourced", "roasted fresh", "nuanced", "ritual"
DON'T use: "premium" (overused), "artisanal" (tired), "luxury", "finest" (sounds stuffy)

## Rules
- Always use active voice
- Contractions are fine ("we're", "you'll", "it's")
- Never use exclamation marks in headlines — let the copy do the work
- Keep sentences short (under 20 words per sentence wherever possible)

## Dos and Don'ts for Ads
DO: lead with discovery ("Find your next favorite roast"), education ("What makes Ethiopian Yirgacheffe different"), community ("Join 8,000 coffee explorers")
DON'T: discount framing ("Save 30%"), urgency pressure ("Act now"), generic lifestyle imagery (generic coffee shop shots)
