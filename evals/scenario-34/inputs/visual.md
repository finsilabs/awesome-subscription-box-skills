# Visual Style — NorthBrew Coffee

## Photography style
- Warm, natural light (golden hour or diffused daylight)
- Rustic surfaces: raw wood, slate, linen
- Coffee as the subject — no people's faces (hands holding a cup is fine)
- No studio white backgrounds — always contextual

## Imagery to avoid
- Generic stock-photo coffee shop interiors
- Overly stylized flat-lays
- Bright, cool-toned environments
- Any imagery that looks "mass-produced"
