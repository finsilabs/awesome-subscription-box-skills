# Instagram Ads Brief for NorthBrew Coffee's Discovery Campaign

## Problem/Feature Description

NorthBrew Coffee is a specialty coffee subscription that ships single-origin beans sourced from small farms worldwide. The marketing team is preparing a cold-traffic Instagram campaign to grow their subscriber base among coffee enthusiasts aged 30-55. The campaign runs for 8 weeks and the monthly budget is $4,000.

The brand's voice and visual identity documents are stored in the `inputs/` directory. These files define the tone of voice, vocabulary rules, and visual style the team has established after two years of building the brand. The marketing lead is strict about maintaining brand consistency in all ad copy.

The campaign goal is to drive first-time subscriptions, with a target CPL (cost per lead / subscriber) under $38. The landing page is the brand's homepage.

Create a full Meta ads creative brief for this campaign. Save the output to `briefs/northbrew-discovery.md`.

## Output Specification

- `briefs/northbrew-discovery.md` — the complete creative brief

The brand vault files are in `inputs/`:
- `inputs/voice.md`
- `inputs/palette.json`
- `inputs/visual.md`
