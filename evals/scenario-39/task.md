# Win-Back Campaign Brief for Lapsed BrushBox Subscribers

## Problem/Feature Description

BrushBox is a monthly art supplies subscription box for adult hobbyists. Over the past 6 months they have acquired about 4,200 paid subscribers, but 800 of them cancelled within the first two months. The marketing team wants to run a paid Meta campaign to bring these lapsed subscribers back — people who have already tried the product and chosen to leave.

Because these potential customers have seen BrushBox ads before and know exactly what the product is, the team does not need broad creative testing. The campaign should be tightly focused on addressing the reasons people cancel (price, perceived value, not using it enough) rather than introducing the product from scratch.

The retargeting audience will be uploaded as a custom audience from the CRM export of cancelled subscribers. The budget is $1,200 for 4 weeks. Target CPA is under $40.

Produce a Meta ads brief for this win-back retargeting campaign. Save it to `briefs/brushbox-winback.md`.
