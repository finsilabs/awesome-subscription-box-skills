# Subscription Box Store Kickoff: Scope Capture Document

## Problem/Feature Description

A new client has reached out about building a subscription box store on Shopify. They have sent a rough brief (see `inputs/client-brief.md`) describing what they want to build, but before any design or development work begins the team needs a complete scope document to ensure nothing is missed during planning. The project lead has asked you to review the client's brief and produce a formal scope capture document that will be sent back to the client to fill in any gaps.

Building a subscription box store requires specific information that many clients don't think to provide upfront. Depending on what's missing, the project could stall mid-build when developers discover they're missing a critical decision — especially around how subscriptions are structured. Your job is to create a thorough, organized scope document that asks for everything the build will need, presented clearly enough that a non-technical client can understand and answer each item.

## Output Specification

Produce a file called `scope-capture.md` that contains the complete scope intake document. It should:
- List every piece of information the build team needs from the client
- Clearly mark any items that are missing from the client brief (so the client knows what to fill in)
- Explain briefly why any critical items are prerequisites before work can begin
- Be organized into logical sections so the client can respond efficiently
