# Client Brief — Petal & Press Subscription Box

**Prepared by:** Mia Thornton, Founder

## What We're Building

Petal & Press is a monthly subscription box for stationery and paper goods lovers. Each box contains 6–8 items curated around a seasonal theme: washi tape, notecards, stickers, mini prints, and one "hero" item (a hardcover journal or art print). We hand-pick items from independent makers and small studios in the UK and Europe.

We've been running a pop-up at craft fairs for two years and have a loyal Instagram following (@petalandpress_uk, ~18k followers). We don't have a website yet — just a Linktree landing page.

## Our Boxes

We want to offer two options:
- A standard box
- A "Deluxe" box with a few extra premium items

We haven't fully decided on pricing yet, but we're thinking £29 and £45.

## When We Want to Launch

We want to launch as soon as possible — ideally within 6 weeks. We're not sure if we'll do a pre-order period or go straight to live.

## Tech Notes

Our Shopify store URL is petalandpress.myshopify.com. We've never set up Shopify before so we're starting completely fresh.
