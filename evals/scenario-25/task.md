# First-Year Email Marketing Plan for BotanicBox

## Problem/Feature Description

BotanicBox is a monthly subscription box for indoor plant enthusiasts, launching in March. They ship a curated selection of seasonal plants, care accessories, and guides to subscribers who choose from three tiers: Seedling ($35/mo), Green Thumb ($55/mo), and Botanist ($85/mo).

The founders have a Klaviyo account set up and a growing pre-launch email waitlist of about 800 people. They've never sent marketing emails before and want a structured plan for their first full year of email marketing. They need to know which campaigns to send and when, how often is appropriate at each stage, and how to think about who receives each send.

The email strategy should feel thoughtful and not spammy — they have a lifestyle brand and want to keep their community tight. They expect to grow their list through paid social and organic content.

## Output Specification

Produce a full-year email marketing calendar as `email-calendar-year1.md` in the current working directory.

The calendar should cover:
- A clear description of how send frequency should evolve across the year as the brand matures
- Seasonal campaigns mapped across all four quarters with approximate dates, channel, intended audience for each send, and purpose
- Rules for what conflicts or blackout periods to respect when scheduling sends
- Clear guidance on what should be handled by automated flows versus one-off campaign sends, so the team understands which items require recurring setup and which need manual scheduling each time
- Notes explaining the reasoning behind cadence choices for BotanicBox specifically
