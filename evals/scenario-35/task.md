# Creative Hook Strategy for FurBox Pet Subscription

## Problem/Feature Description

FurBox is a monthly subscription box for dog owners, delivering a curated selection of toys, treats, and accessories selected by veterinary-approved contributors. The brand is preparing its first Meta cold-traffic campaign targeting dog owners in the US aged 25-45. They have a modest testing budget of $2,500 and want to run creative tests to find which message angle resonates best before scaling.

The campaign objective is acquiring new subscribers at a CPA under $55. The marketing director wants to ensure the brief covers a diverse range of creative angles so the team can run meaningful A/B tests in the first two weeks of the campaign.

Create a Meta ads creative brief for FurBox's subscriber acquisition campaign. The brief should give the creative team enough distinct hook concepts to test meaningfully. Save the output to `briefs/furbox-acquisition.md`.

## Output Specification

- `briefs/furbox-acquisition.md` — the full creative brief
